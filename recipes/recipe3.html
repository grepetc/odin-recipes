Breakfast Quesadillas

Description

Easy to make breakfast quesadilla, consisting of bacon, eggs, and cheese.

Serving size: 1 people

Estimated time to make: ~10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 flour tortilla (~8 inch in diameter)
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 slices of bacon (sliced)
  • salt and pepper
  • 1/4 cup of shredded cheese

Steps

  1. Lay the bacon strips over a pan and heat the pan over medium heat.
  2. Cook the 2 bacon strips for about 4 minutes per side or until brown and crispy.
  3. Once done, set the bacon aside pour most of the left oil away, leaving a small coating then reduce the heat to low.
  4. In a separate cup, break the two eggs into the cup and add a pinch of salt and pepper. Beat the eggs with fork.
  5. Add the beaten the eggs evenly onto the pan and cook on low heat until the eggs are nearly cooked.
  6. Sprinkle about 2 Tbsp of shredded cheese over the eggs and then cover with the tortilla.
  7. Use a spatula to flip the egg over with the tortilla on the bottom and egg on top.
  8. Sprinkle another 2 Tbsp of cheese and the sliced bacon onto the pan over the eggs.
  9. Fold the tortilla half and sautee on both sides until golden brown, then turn off the heat.
  10. Remove the quesadilla and serve warm.
