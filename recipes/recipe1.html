French Toast

Description

Dating back to the Roman empire, French toast is a sweet breakfast dish that can be served with honey, syrup, butter, whipped cream, etc.

Serving size: 2 people

Estimated time to make: ~10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 4 slices of bread
  • 1/4 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Beat the eggs, add milk then whisk until the mixture has a nice cream color.
  2. Add the sugar, cinnamon, and vanilla extract and continue whisking.
  3. Pour the egg mixture into a wider pan that can fit a bread slice.
  4. Let the slices of bread evenly soak into the egg mixture(coat both sides of the bread slices).
  5. Heat a medium sized pan using medium heat and drop the slices onto the pan when ready.
  6. Let the bread slices cook on the pan for about 2 minutes each side.
  7. Serve warm with maple syrup and butter.
